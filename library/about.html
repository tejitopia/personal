About

b. 2000, Sydney, Australia

Hi, my name is Jet. My full name is Jet Lee Raymond Williams, hence the LR in my domain. I’m a 24 year old Aboriginal Australian from Sydney. I’m an artist and writer trying to find my place in the world.

Here I share my experiences, views and things I find interesting with my community of like minded friends. Some of the things I’m interested in include: art, design, music, technology, sci-fi, the future, history, culture, crypto, spirituality, mindfulness, business, film, reading and many, many, more weird an intricate things you will come to learn about.
