Principles for Life

Jan 3rd, 2024

A list of guiding principles for life by Jet Williams

  • Use what you have
  • Never hold in a compliment
  • Stay curious
  • Challenge yourself
  • Listen more than you talk
  • Embrace the unknown
  • Think for yourself
  • Don't be afraid to ask for help
  • Good habits are contagious
  • Learn to let go of things
  • Don't forget to play
  • Progress over perfection
  • Stand up for yourself
  • Tell the truth
  • Talk to strangers
  • How you react is everything
  • Accept your emotions
  • Know when to roll up your sleeves
  • True change never happens over night
  • Know when to rest
  • Always be creating
  • Always be learning
  • Pessimism is a virus
  • Keep your word
  • Believe
  • Loss and gain are the same
  • Life is in the grey
  • Say what you mean
  • Avoid gossip
  • Know when to break the rules

NOTE: Principles shouldn't always be followed. There is always an exception. Know when to break the rules.
