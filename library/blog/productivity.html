Productivity

Thoughts on productivity written by Jet Williams on Jan 11th, 2024

I wrote this list as a basic framework to help increase the productivity in my life. These principles are merely guidelines and the opposite of each idea is almost also true in some cases.

  • Eat the frog first
  • Accept that everything is a work in progress
  • Get going then get good
  • Brick by brick
  • Laugh at perfection
  • Finished work creates more finished work
  • Fail more but learn from your mistakes
  • Write to organise your thoughts and ideas
  • Allocate time dedicated to thinking about ideas
  • Curate your content consumtion using RSS feeds
  • Write lots of lists
  • Use a text to speech generator to test how your writing reads
  • If you get an idea write it down immediately
  • Start what you finish but also know when to quit

Some ideas in my list that have been adapted from this YouTube video, Sigil Wen and Sam Altman.
